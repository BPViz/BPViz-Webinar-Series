Tableau Online Workshop Environment
Workspace in the Tableau Online platform have been created for each workshop participant.
Once you create your TableauID you will be able to access your folder in the Tableau Online environment.
You will have access to the following folders: your folder saved with your name, and your group folder.
Only you will have access to your individual folder. Access to the group folder will be shared by group members.
Workshop organizers have access to ALL folders in the Tableau Online Environment.
